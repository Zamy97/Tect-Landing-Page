Instant Contact Saves Lives

Tect accelerates and simplifies the communication process from schools to emergency personnel in high stakes situations. It’s as simple as pulling out the app, confirming your location, and sending your emergency instantly to the authorities who matter most. Rest easy knowing help is on the way.

Simple and Silent

Ever been in an emergency situation where you can’t get on the phone? What if you could send your location and message to 911 in seconds with the press of a button?

Details Matter

Our app uses indoor mapping to get your precise location inside a building. This way, emergency support knows exactly where to go.

Speak or Type

Using natural language processing, we determine the specifics of the emergency and notify the most relevent parties: Police, Administratos, Firemen, EMS.
